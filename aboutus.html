At DR. RVR IAS Academy, We Don’t Just Coach — We Create Officers.

Established in 2004, DR. RVR IAS Academy stands tall as one of Tamil Nadu’s most respected and result-oriented institutions for civil services and competitive exam preparation. With over two decades of unwavering commitment, we have empowered thousands of aspirants to secure prestigious positions in Central and State Government sectors - turning aspirations into accomplishments.

Led by the visionary educator Dr. R.V. Rangarajan, our academy is grounded in values of discipline, dedication, and academic excellence. His decision to pursue teaching over a government post highlights our core belief that true success lies in uplifting others.

Our offline-first classroom model ensures personal attention, structured mentoring, and continuous motivation. From UPSC IAS/IPS/IRS/IFS to TNPSC Group I, II, IV, SSC (CGL/CHSL), Banking (IBPS/SBI/RRB), TRB (TET/PG/ UG), and English enrichment programs, we offer specialized coaching for over 100+ competitive exams, tailored to school finishers, graduates, and working professionals.

We emphasize concept clarity, strategic preparation, real time assessments, and individual performance tracking. At DR. RVR IAS Academy, we believe that “Education is Service, and Teaching is a Sacred Duty.”

This is not just a coaching center it's a launchpad for transformation. Our students are now proud Government Officers, VAOs, Bank Officers, and Government Teachers making a meaningful difference across Tamil Nadu and beyond.
